Program Overview

Theological Education
at Every Level

The Old Catholic University offers programs of theological education and ministerial formation designed to support the spiritual, intellectual, and pastoral development of individuals serving the Church. Programs emphasize the integration of theological scholarship, spiritual formation, pastoral leadership, and ecclesial service.

The University currently offers programs at the undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral levels, drawing students from many nations and ecclesial traditions who seek serious formation in the apostolic faith of the Holy Undivided Church.

Degree Programs

Sacred Theology
& Ministry Degrees

Undergraduate Level

Bachelor's Degree

Bachelor of Old Catholic Theology

B.O.C.Th.

The foundational undergraduate degree of the University. Provides students with a structured introduction to Sacred Scripture, dogmatic theology, patristics, Church history, liturgical theology, sacramental theology, and Christian philosophy. This degree forms the theological and spiritual foundation for further graduate study or direct ministerial service.

Ideal for

Lay faithful seeking deeper theological formation · Candidates discerning ordination · Those beginning formal theological study within the tradition of Western Orthodoxy.

Graduate Level

Master's Degrees

Master of Divinity

M.Div.

The principal graduate degree for candidates preparing for ordained ministry. Advanced formation in theology, pastoral ministry, liturgical studies, and sacramental practice. The M.Div. prepares ministers who are theologically grounded, spiritually mature, and pastorally wise.

Master of Ministry Leadership

M.M.L.

An advanced ecclesiastical degree designed for clergy, lay leaders, and church leaders seeking formation in pastoral leadership, ecclesial governance, spiritual formation, and ministry development. Suitable for those already in active ministry seeking deeper preparation.

Doctoral Level

Doctoral Degrees

Doctor of Ministry

D.Min.

An advanced professional doctorate for experienced clergy and ministers seeking deeper pastoral, theological, and ministerial development. Designed for those already serving in ordained ministry who wish to develop their theological reflection and pastoral practice at the highest level.

Doctor of Sacred Theology

S.Th.D.

The University's highest academic research degree, intended for theologians, scholars, and experienced clergy engaged in advanced theological research and scholarly contribution to the life of the Church. This degree represents the pinnacle of theological formation at The Old Catholic University.

Ecclesiastical Purpose

All degrees awarded by The Old Catholic University are granted for religious and ecclesiastical purposes — including ordination preparation, theological education, spiritual formation, and ministerial development. These degrees do not lead to state licensure and are not represented as secular professional qualifications.
