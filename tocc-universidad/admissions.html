Application Process

Your Path to
Theological Formation

The Old Catholic University welcomes candidates discerning a call to ordained ministry, as well as clergy, lay leaders, and students from other ecclesial traditions who desire serious theological study within the context of the apostolic faith of the Holy Undivided Church.

I

Discern Your Call

Pray and reflect on your vocation. Whether you are discerning ordained ministry or seeking deeper theological formation as a lay faithful, the University welcomes you to begin this journey.

II

Choose Your Program

Select the degree that corresponds to your formation needs — from the foundational B.O.C.Th. to the advanced S.Th.D. in Sacred Theology. Our Academic Dean is available to assist.

III

Submit Your Application

Complete the application with your personal and ecclesiastical information. Include a statement of purpose describing your vocational discernment and theological interests.

IV

Enter Formation

Upon acceptance, begin your journey of study, prayer, and service within the living tradition of the apostolic faith of the Holy Undivided Church.

Who We Welcome

A University Open
to All Seekers

Ordination Candidates

Those discerning a call to the Diaconate, Priesthood, or Episcopal orders within The Old Catholic Church receive formation through the Seminary of St. Matthew of South Mimms.

Clergy & Ministers

Ordained clergy and ministers from any ecclesial tradition seeking advanced theological formation, continuing education, or preparation for deeper ecclesial service.

Lay Faithful

Lay members of the Church seeking to deepen their knowledge of the apostolic faith, serve through teaching and catechesis, or cultivate a deeper spiritual life grounded in prayer and tradition.
